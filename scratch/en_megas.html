How to Get Mega Stones

Buy Them in the Shop

You can buy Mega Stones at the Shop for 2000 VP. You can earn VP by participating in battles!

Items List

Battle Pass Rewards


You can also receive Mega Stones from the Battle Pass. The confirmed Mega Stones available in the Season 1 Battle Pass are Emboarite and Dragoninite.

All Battle Pass Rewards

Pokemon HOME Transfer Gifts


You can receive the Greninjite, Chesnaughtite, Floettite, and Delphoxite if you have transfer the corresponding Pokemon from Pokemon Legends: Z-A to Pokemon Champions via Pokemon HOME.

Mega Stones Collaboration Event

Obtain from Battle Tutorial

You can also obtain Mega Stones by completing the Mega Evolution Battle Tutorial.

You can access Battle Tutorials in the Train screen in the main menu.

How to Use Mega Stones

Equip to Pokemon and Mega Evolve

Mega Stones allow Pokemon to Mega Evolve in battle. To Mega Evolve a Pokemon, you need to first equip the corresponding Mega Stone to a Pokemon as a Held Item, then press R before attacking with that Pokemon in battle.

Keep in mind that only one Pokemon can use a Mega Evolution per battle.
